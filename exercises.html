Yoga Exercises

Practice these yoga poses daily to improve flexibility, strength, and mental peace. Each asana includes steps and benefits.

Dhanurasana (Bow Pose)

Steps:
  • Lie flat on your stomach, keep feet hip-width apart, arms by your side.
  • Fold your knees, hold ankles with your hands.
  • Inhale, lift chest and legs, pulling ankles upward.
  • Look forward and hold for 20-30 seconds.
  • Exhale, release slowly.
Advantages:
  • Strengthens back and abdominal muscles.
  • Improves posture and flexibility.
  • Relieves stress and fatigue.

Matsyasana (Fish Pose)

Steps:
  • Lie on your back with legs extended.
  • Place hands under hips, palms down.
  • Inhale, lift chest, arch back, rest head crown on floor.
  • Hold for 20-30 seconds while breathing deeply.
Advantages:
  • Stretches chest, throat, and abdomen.
  • Improves posture and lung capacity.
  • Relieves tension in neck and shoulders.

Vrikshasana (Tree Pose)

Steps:
  • Stand straight, shift weight onto left leg.
  • Place right foot on left inner thigh.
  • Join palms in Namaste, balance and stand tall.
  • Hold for 30-60 seconds, then switch sides.
Advantages:
  • Improves balance and concentration.
  • Strengthens legs and spine.
  • Enhances focus and stability.

Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)

Steps:
  • Lie on stomach, place palms near chest.
  • Inhale, lift head and chest up, elbows bent.
  • Look upward, keep lower body pressed down.
  • Hold for 20-30 seconds, then release.
Advantages:
  • Strengthens spine and back muscles.
  • Opens chest and lungs.
  • Relieves stress and fatigue.

Chakrasana (Wheel Pose)

Steps:
  • Lie on back, bend knees, feet flat on floor.
  • Place palms under shoulders, fingers pointing backward.
  • Inhale, press palms and feet, lift hips and chest.
  • Hold 15-30 seconds, then release.
Advantages:
  • Strengthens arms, legs, spine.
  • Increases flexibility of the back.
  • Energizes the body and mind.

Halasana (Plough Pose)

Steps:
  • Lie on back, arms beside body.
  • Lift legs over head until toes touch floor.
  • Keep back straight, hold 20-30 seconds.
Advantages:
  • Relieves stress and fatigue.
  • Stretches shoulders and spine.
  • Improves digestion and blood circulation.

Natarajasana (Lord of the Dance Pose)

Steps:
  • Stand straight, bend right knee backward.
  • Hold ankle with right hand, stretch left arm forward.
  • Balance, stretch back leg upward.
  • Hold for 20-30 seconds, then switch.
Advantages:
  • Improves balance and posture.
  • Strengthens legs and core.
  • Enhances flexibility of spine and hips.

Padmasana (Lotus Pose)

Steps:
  • Sit with legs extended, bend right knee, place foot on left thigh.
  • Repeat with left leg.
  • Place hands on knees in Gyan Mudra.
  • Sit with spine straight, breathe deeply.
Advantages:
  • Calms the mind, reduces stress.
  • Improves digestion.
  • Increases flexibility of hips and knees.

Paschimottanasana (Seated Forward Bend)

Steps:
  • Sit with legs extended, feet together.
  • Inhale, raise arms overhead.
  • Exhale, bend forward, hold toes.
  • Rest head on knees, hold 20-30 seconds.
Advantages:
  • Stretches spine, shoulders, hamstrings.
  • Relieves stress and depression.
  • Improves digestion.

Sarvangasana (Shoulder Stand)

Steps:
  • Lie flat, lift legs and hips upward.
  • Support back with hands, keep legs straight.
  • Chin should rest on chest.
  • Hold for 30-60 seconds.
Advantages:
  • Improves blood circulation.
  • Strengthens shoulders and core.
  • Reduces stress and anxiety.

Shalabhasana (Locust Pose)

Steps:
  • Lie on stomach, arms beside thighs.
  • Inhale, lift both legs and chest upward.
  • Keep arms straight, hold 20-30 seconds.
Advantages:
  • Strengthens back and glutes.
  • Improves digestion.
  • Relieves lower back pain.

Supta Vajrasana (Reclined Thunderbolt Pose)

Steps:
  • Kneel down, sit on heels.
  • Slowly recline back, place elbows and back on floor.
  • Place hands on thighs, breathe deeply.
Advantages:
  • Strengthens thighs and ankles.
  • Improves digestion.
  • Relieves stress and fatigue.

Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation)

Steps:
  • Perform a sequence of 12 postures starting with Pranamasana (Prayer pose).
  • Include forward bend, plank, cobra, and mountain pose.
  • Complete both sides for one round.
Advantages:
  • Full body workout improving flexibility and strength.
  • Improves digestion and circulation.
  • Relieves stress and energizes body.

Uttanasana (Standing Forward Bend)

Steps:
  • Stand straight, inhale and raise arms up.
  • Exhale, bend forward, hands touch floor beside feet.
  • Hold for 20-30 seconds, breathe deeply.
Advantages:
  • Stretches hamstrings and spine.
  • Relieves stress, anxiety, and fatigue.
  • Improves blood flow to the brain.
