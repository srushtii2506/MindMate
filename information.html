What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It influences how we think, feel, and behave in daily life. Conditions such as stress, depression, and anxiety can affect mental health. Just like physical health, mental health is important at every stage of life.

"Your mental health is just as important as your physical health."

Sharing with loved ones reduces stress. Talking is strength, not weakness.

Ask for Help

Counselors, hotlines, or support groups can provide relief when overwhelmed.
