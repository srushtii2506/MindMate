Diet for Better Mental Health

Your mental well-being is deeply connected to what you eat. A balanced diet nourishes your brain, reduces stress, and keeps your mood stable. Explore brain-boosting foods and a special Mental Health Food Pyramid below.

Foods That Boost Mental Wellness

Omega-3 Rich Foods

Salmon, walnuts, and flaxseeds enhance brain function and reduce depression symptoms.

Leafy Greens

Spinach, kale, and broccoli provide folate and antioxidants that reduce stress and boost memory.

Whole Grains

Brown rice, oats, and quinoa release glucose slowly, keeping energy & mood stable.

Probiotics

Yogurt, kefir, and fermented foods improve gut health, reducing anxiety & stress.

Colorful Berries

Blueberries and strawberries are rich in antioxidants that protect brain cells.

Hydration & Green Tea

Green tea contains L-theanine for focus & relaxation. Hydration keeps the brain sharp.
